CUCEK Photography Club | Official Website

Welcome to the official website of the CUCEK Photography Club (CPC), the creative heartbeat of Cochin University College of Engineering Kuttanad (CUCEK). Our club is a dedicated community of photography enthusiasts, visual storytellers, and creative artists committed to documenting the vibrant life of our campus and the stunning landscapes of the Kuttanad region.

Our Mission and Activities

The CUCEK Photography Club provides a professional platform for students to explore their passion for photography and cinematography. We conduct regular technical workshops covering camera operations, lighting theory, composition, and professional post-processing using industrial tools. Our activities include organized photo walks through the scenic backwaters of Alappuzha, campus-wide photography competitions, and annual exhibitions that showcase student talent.

Official Event Coverage: Aaravam and Beyond

As the official media wing, CPC provides comprehensive coverage of all major university events. From the grand cultural spectacle of Aaravam to sports meets and technical symposiums, our team ensures every moment is preserved. Our members gain real-world experience in event photography, photojournalism, and social media content creation.

Photography Galleries and Portfolio

Explore our extensive digital galleries featuring high-quality student work across diverse genres: Landscape Photography, capturing the serene beauty of Kerala; Portraiture, focusing on the people and personalities of CUCEK; Street and Documentary, recording the daily rhythms of campus life; and Macro Photography, exploring the finer details of nature.

Join the CPC Family

The CUCEK Photography Club is open to all students, from absolute beginners with smartphones to advanced photographers with professional DSLR and mirrorless systems. By joining CPC, you become part of a legacy of creative excellence, gaining access to peer learning, mentorship, and a platform to build a professional photography portfolio.

Meet the Team and Alumni

Our club is led by a dedicated team of student coordinators and faculty advisors. We take pride in our alumni network, with many former members now excelling in professional photography, filmmaking, and creative design industries worldwide.
