CUCEK Photography Club | Official Website

Welcome to the official website of the CUCEK Photography Club (CPC), the creative heartbeat of Cochin University College of Engineering Kuttanad. Our club is dedicated to the art of visual storytelling, capturing the unique essence of campus life, student events, and the breathtaking beauty of the Kuttanad region.

Our Mission and Activities

The CUCEK Photography Club provides a vibrant platform for students to explore their passion for photography. We conduct regular workshops on camera techniques, lighting, and post-processing, alongside organized photo walks and campus-wide competitions. Our goal is to nurture the creative talents of students and provide an archival record of the college's history through high-quality imagery.

Explore Our Photography Galleries

Dive into our extensive galleries featuring student work across various genres: landscape, portrait, architecture, and street photography. We provide comprehensive event coverage for major college festivals such as Aaravam, sports meets, and technical symposiums.

Join the Community

Whether you are a beginner with a smartphone or a seasoned pro with a DSLR, the CUCEK Photography Club welcomes all students who share a love for capturing moments. Join us to build your portfolio, collaborate on creative projects, and be part of an elite team of student photographers.
